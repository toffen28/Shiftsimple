# ShiftSimple 30-Day Guerrilla Marketing Plan

Target: Norwegian restaurant and café owners (1-20 staff).
Focus: Free channels, direct outreach, and community engagement.

## Week 1: Foundation & Direct Outreach
*   **Day 1:** Lead Generation. Identify 50 target cafés/restaurants in major Norwegian cities (Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim, Stavanger) using Google Maps/Yellow Pages.
*   **Day 2-4:** Personalized Outreach. Send cold emails using the provided template. Focus on 15-20 leads per day.
*   **Day 5:** Community Immersion. Join the 10 identified Facebook groups. Read recent posts to understand current frustrations.
*   **Day 6:** Soft Launch. Post a conversational question (Post 1) in 2-3 groups to spark discussion about scheduling pains.
*   **Day 7:** Follow-up. Reply to any email responses and engagement on Facebook posts.

## Week 2: Social Proof & Local Presence
*   **Day 8:** Reddit Launch. Post in r/norge (Norwegian) and monitor for feedback.
*   **Day 9:** The "Relatable Pain" Post. Share Post 2 in 3 Facebook groups.
*   **Day 10:** Research local business hubs or co-working spaces where café owners might gather or get information.
*   **Day 11-13:** Feet on the Street. Visit 5-10 local cafés in person. Ask for the manager, keep it brief, and leave a card/flyer with a QR code to the 14-day trial.
*   **Day 14:** Weekly Review. Check trial signups and website traffic. Adjust messaging based on feedback.

## Week 3: Scaling & Content
*   **Day 15:** Global Feedback. Post in r/entrepreneur and r/SaaS to gain visibility and potential "Product Hunt" style momentum.
*   **Day 16:** The "Efficiency" Post. Share Post 3 in the remaining Facebook groups.
*   **Day 17-19:** Second Wave Outreach. Identify and email 50 new leads.
*   **Day 20:** Video Content. Record a 60-second screen recording of "How to create a weekly schedule in ShiftSimple" and share on LinkedIn and Facebook.
*   **Day 21:** Value Add. Comment on 5-10 posts in industry groups, offering genuine advice or tips (not just pitching).

## Week 4: Closing & Optimization
*   **Day 22:** The "Feature Highlight" Post. Share Post 4 focusing on the PDF export and professional look.
*   **Day 23-25:** Warm DM Outreach. Message individuals who interacted with previous posts or expressed interest in scheduling tools.
*   **Day 26:** The "Tester" Post. Share Post 5 offering a "feedback-for-trial" deal to get more active users.
*   **Day 27-29:** Final Follow-ups. Re-contact leads from Week 1 who haven't responded yet.
*   **Day 30:** Performance Analysis. Review trial signups, trial-to-paid conversion (if applicable), and group engagement. Plan Month 2.
